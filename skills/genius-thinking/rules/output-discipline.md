# Output Discipline

- Optimize for distinct idea clusters, not a wall of loosely different bullets.
- Dedup near-identical ideas before ranking them.
- Make prioritization criteria explicit: expected upside, feasibility, speed to test, and defensibility.
- Favor concrete mechanisms over vague slogans.
- Keep the strongest shortlist small enough to compare clearly.
- Use evidence or fresh examples only when they materially improve prioritization.
