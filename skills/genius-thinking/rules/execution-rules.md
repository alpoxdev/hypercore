# Execution Rules

## Required inputs

Collect these before ideation:

- the problem or opportunity statement
- the target user or market
- the intended outcome or decision
- hard constraints such as time, team, budget, or regulation

If key constraints are missing, proceed only with explicit assumptions.

## Operating sequence

1. Restate the problem and pick 2-3 frameworks that match it.
2. Reframe the problem before generating ideas.
3. Generate a diverse set of options across at least 3 distinct directions.
4. Remove duplicates and thin variations.
5. Prioritize with explicit evidence, assumptions, and next tests.

## Anti-patterns

- Do not pad the answer with mandatory character counts.
- Do not dump every framework when only a few are relevant.
- Do not score ideas without explaining the tradeoff.
- Do not treat quantity alone as creativity.
